How to make a quesadilla

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Mexican Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Oil
  • Your choice of seasoning

Directions

  1. Heat up pan on a stove top
  2. Warm up oil on pan
  3. Put ground beef on pan once it has reached medium heat
  4. Season beef once it begins to change color
  5. Mix beef in pan to ensure it is cooked entirely
  6. Once beef is fully cooked place it on the side
  7. Lower heat on pan
  8. Once the heat is on medium-low, place tortilla on pan
  9. Spread Mexican cheese on one half of the tortilla
  10. Once the cheese has begun to melt, add ground beef on top of the cheese
  11. Fold tortilla in half, and warm up both sides
  12. Once both sides are warmed up, take quesadilla off of the pan
  13. Enjoy!
